Frequently Asked Questions

Honest answers to typical questions about our premium financial advisory services.

What is the minimum investment required to start wealth planning?

At Pinnacle Wealth Advisors, we customize plans for clients at different life stages. For comprehensive portfolio management services, we recommend a minimum investible asset size of ₹25 Lakhs, while our standard advisory starts with custom monthly SIP roadmaps for younger professionals.

How do you charge for your wealth advisory services?

We offer structured fee models depending on client needs: either a flat annual planning fee for comprehensive blueprint creation or an asset-under-advisory (AUA) percentage fee for ongoing portfolio management. There are no hidden charges.

What is the difference between direct mutual funds and regular mutual funds?

Direct mutual funds have a lower expense ratio because they do not include distributor commissions. Pinnacle Wealth Advisors is a client-first advisory that focuses on optimizing returns, recommending direct plans where appropriate alongside detailed rebalancing strategies.

Are you registered with SEBI?

Yes, our advisors are certified financial experts operating under strict SEBI guidelines to maintain absolute integrity, fiduciary responsibility, and transparency in all financial recommendations.

How often will we review my investment portfolio?

We conduct comprehensive portfolio reviews quarterly to analyze performance, asset allocations, and check if rebalancing is required due to market shifts or updates in your life goals.

Can you help with NRI tax savings strategies in India?

Absolutely. We specialize in NRI financial services, helping you navigate NRE/NRO accounts, tax saving under Section 80C, double taxation avoidance treaties (DTAA), and custom real estate or mutual fund investment planning.

What happens during the initial free 30-minute consultation?

The discovery call is a no-obligation session where we understand your current financial situation, primary pain points, future goals, and see if our customized advisory services are the right fit for your needs.

Do you provide insurance advisory along with investment planning?

Yes. A secure financial plan requires risk protection. We conduct a thorough gap analysis of your life and health insurance covers to ensure you and your family are fully shielded against emergencies.
