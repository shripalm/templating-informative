The Artist

PAL by Rahul Jagani

A premier wedding and fine art photography studio based in Surat, Gujarat, guided by veteran artist Rahul Jagani.

For over two decades, Rahul Jagani has been capturing the delicate highlights of life: stills, shades, and stories. The studio's work blends the clean layouts of high-fashion editorials with the emotional truth of documentary photography, resulting in eye-soothing, evergreen visual art.

As an artist recognized on major national platforms, Rahul has shot celebrity tours and events (including the Salman Khan Da-Bangg Tour and concerts with Shaan and the late KK) and has won awards on all major platforms, including the WeddingSutra Awards, GIWA (Great Indian Wedding Awards - ICWF), and EMF Global Awards.

Answering Your Questions

Here are a few answers to general inquiries about timelines, destinations, styling, and how we coordinate booking details.

Our style is a curated blend of fine-art editorial and organic storytelling—stills, shades, and stories. We prioritize soft, natural light, warm beige/ivory tone palettes, and focus on capturing candid expressions rather than highly posed standard frames.

Yes, absolutely. We love shooting in unfamiliar territories. We regularly shoot in Surat, Udaipur, Goa, Mumbai, and are available for international celebrity bookings. Travel and accommodation fees are calculated based on the location details.

We send a sneak-peek gallery of 15-20 images within 7 days of the shoot. The complete edited high-resolution digital archive is typically delivered via a private link within 4 to 6 weeks, depending on the project scope.
