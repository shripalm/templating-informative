HAVE QUESTIONS?

Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know about parking, reservations, dietary preferences, and amenities.

Do you have parking facilities?

Yes, we offer complimentary valet parking for all our guests at the Premium High Street entrance, along with secure basement parking for two-wheelers and cars.

Is the WiFi fast enough for video calls?

Absolutely. We provide high-speed gigabit fiber internet (speeds up to 300 Mbps) with multiple node access routers to ensure strong connectivity across indoor and outdoor spaces.

Can I reserve a table in advance?

Yes, table reservations are recommended on weekends and during brunch peak hours (10 AM - 2 PM). You can reserve a spot via the "Book Table" button on this website or by calling us.

Are pets allowed inside the cafe?

We are pet-friendly! Pets are welcome on our outdoor botanical patio. We have water bowls, custom pet mats, and dog-friendly treats available on request.

Do you offer vegan and gluten-free options?

Yes, our menu features dedicated vegan coffee milk choices (oat and almond), vegan brunch alternatives, and gluten-free botanical desserts. Please notify your server of any allergy specifics.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI payments (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), net banking, and standard digital wallets.
